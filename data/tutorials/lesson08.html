Lesson 8: Manipulating Objects

Accessing Transformations

Use the Transformations tab in the left panel or right-click selected objects.

Screenshot: tutorial_08_transform_panel.png

Translation, Rotation, and Scaling

Translation
Move objects along X, Y, or Z by entering DX, DY, DZ values.
Screenshot: tutorial_08_translate.png
Rotation
Rotate around axes (degrees). Applied in order: X, then Y, then Z.
Screenshot: tutorial_08_rotate.png
Scale
Factors >1 enlarge; <1 shrink. Uniform values maintain proportions.
Screenshot: tutorial_08_scale.png

Duplicating

Create copies via Right-Click → Duplicate. Use transformations to separate the copy from the original.

Screenshot: tutorial_08_duplicate.png
Baking Transformations: Clicking 'Bake' permanently applies transformations to mesh vertices. This cannot be undone!
